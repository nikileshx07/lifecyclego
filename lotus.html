Lifecycle of Ant

The lotus life cycle begins with a seed germinating in muddy water. A strong stem emerges, bearing large, round leaves that float on the water's surface. A flower bud develops, eventually blooming into a beautiful lotus flower. After pollination, the flower withers, and a seed pod forms. The pod matures, releasing seeds that sink to the bottom of the pond, ready to start the cycle anew.
