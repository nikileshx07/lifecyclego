The Lifecycle of Lily

A lily's life starts as a tiny seed, planted in the earth.
It sprouts and grows, nourished by sunlight and water.
As it matures, it blooms into a beautiful flower, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
After blooming, the flower fades, and the plant produces seeds, starting the cycle anew.
