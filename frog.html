The Lifecycle of a Frog

Eggs: Frogs start their life as eggs. The female frog lays her eggs in water, usually in a cluster or mass.
Tadpole: After the eggs hatch, they become tadpoles. Tadpoles are little fish-like creatures with a tail and gills, and they live in the water.
Tadpole with Legs: As the tadpole grows, it starts to grow legs. First, the back legs appear, and then the front legs. The tadpole also develops lungs to breathe air.
Froglet: The tadpole's tail shrinks, and it becomes a froglet. The froglet looks like a small frog but still needs to stay near water.
Adult Frog: Finally, the froglet grows into an adult frog. The frog is fully developed with strong legs for jumping, and it can live on land or in water.
