The Lifecycle of Cat

1. Kittenhood (Birth to 6 Months):Rapid growth and development.Learns social skills from mother and littermates.Begins exploring and playing.Gets their first vaccinations.
2. Junior (6 Months to 2 Years):Reaches full size and sexual maturity.Important to spay or neuter to prevent unwanted litters.Continues to learn and explore.May develop behavioral quirks.
3. Prime (3 to 6 Years): and mentally at their peak.Energetic and playful.Regular vet checkups are important.
4. Mature (7 to 10 Yeaars):May start to slow down and gain weight.Important to monitor their health and adjust their diet.Regular vet checkups are essential.
5. Senior (10+ Years):May experience age-related health issues.Needs special care and attention.Regular vet checkups are crucial.
