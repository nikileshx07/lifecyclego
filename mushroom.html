The Lifecycle of Mushroom

Spore Germination: Spores land in a suitable environment and germinate into hyphae.
Mycelium Formation: Hyphae grow and branch, forming a network called mycelium.
Fruiting Body Development: Under favorable conditions, the mycelium produces a fruiting body, the mushroom we see.
Spore Release: The mushroom releases spores from its gills or pores, starting the cycle anew.
