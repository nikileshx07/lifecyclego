The Lifecycle of Horse

From a tiny foal to a majestic stallion, a horse's life unfolds in stages.
It begins as a newborn, dependent on its mother for nourishment and protection.
As it grows, it learns to stand, walk, and run, exploring the world around it.
Reaching adulthood, it develops its full strength and grace, ready to serve humans or roam freely.
Finally, as years pass, it enters its senior years, its spirit enduring even as its body shows signs of age.
