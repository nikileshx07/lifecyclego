The Lifecycle of Chrysanthemum

From a tiny seed, a chrysanthemum sprouts, reaching for the sun.
It grows into a bushy plant, adorned with vibrant flowers in various hues.
After blooming, the flowers fade, and the plant produces seeds.
These seeds, scattered by the wind, start the cycle anew, promising a burst of color in the next season.
