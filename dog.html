The Lifecycle of Dog

Puppyhood (0-1 year): Rapid growth and development. Learns basic skills and behaviors. Needs frequent feeding, exercise, and socialization.
Adolescence (1-2 years): Physical growth slows down. Behavioral challenges may arise. Important to continue training and socialization.
Adulthood (2-7 years): Physically mature and energetic. Requires regular exercise and mental stimulation. Routine vet checkups are crucial.
Senior (7+ years): May experience age-related health issues. Needs special care and attention. Regular vet checkups are essential.
